Q & A


What is the venue like?

The venue is a farm in Orange, VA. The ceremony and cocktail hour will be held outdoors and the reception in the barn. There is a lot of grassy terrain so please wear suitable shoes!
What is the dress code?

Semi-formal/cocktail attire.
Is there parking on site?

Yes! The venue allows parking on a field in the back. There will be parking attendants available to assist you.
What is the schedule?

The ceremony will start at 5 PM and there will be a cocktail hour directly after. The reception will start around 7 PM and must end by 11 PM.
Will there be alcohol?

Yes- we will have an open bar with two signature mixed drinks as well as a variety of beer and wine available.
Can I bring a guest?

Plus ones will be listed on the invitations. Please don't bring anyone unless your invitation includes a guest! We have a lot of people coming and want to make sure there's enough food and drink for everyone. If you think there was a mistake with your invitation just let us know and we'll sort it out.
What will the food be like?

Dinner will be buffet style with BBQ pulled pork and bruschetta chicken as the entree options along with a variety of sides to choose from.
